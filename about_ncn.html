About NCN

The National CSR Network (NCN) is an initiative of Strategic CSR Alliance which comprises a panel of professionals from industry to assist companies in India to fulfil their CSR quotient and facilitate implementation of social and environmental development projects. The NCN is set up with a vision to empower corporates / companies in adopting Meaningful Social Development. The Network provides companies a platform to share and learn best practices in the field. The services of the Network are based on the individual needs and capabilities of each company. NCN's services strictly comply with the Government of India's CSR legislation as well as with global standards and guidelines
