NETWORKING FORUM

NCN Networking Forum provides a platform for influencing, educating and collaborating, on issues such as CSR and Sustainability, primary health, education and literacy, environment, skills development and so on. It helps companies in arriving at collective solution on key issues with regard to identification of projects to assessment of impacts so as to ensure its long term sustainability. Each Forum is being chaired by senior officials/CEOs representing various industries with representations of companies interested in specific interventions on CSR. Each Forum will meet once in a quarter and would track progress made by the companies. NCN, as a catalyst, would be available for any companies for integrating CSR in companies. There is no fee for attending the Forum meetings.

CSR and sustainability

Key challenges on Health

• Growing population
• Poor hygiene and living conditions
• Absence of infrastructure
• Non-availability of doctors, nurses and paramedics
• Inaccessibility of healthcare services
• Limited Affordability
• Low awareness

Key challenges on Education and literacy

• Poverty
• Early marriage
• Social rituals and customs
• Social attitude
• Lack of awareness and resources
• Non-availability of infrastructure

Key challenges on Environment

• Population growth
• Land/soil degradation
• Depletion of ground water
• Loss of eco-system
• Poor management of waste

Key challenges on Skills Development

• Poor linkage between skills and employment
• Absence of trainers/Lack of infrastructure
• Skill gap of formal and informal sector
• Multiplicity of institutional framework

Key Challenges of CSR and Sustainability

• Lack of Awareness amongst companies
• Lack of transparency
• Availability of only few Well Organized Non-Governmental Organizations
• Narrow Perception Towards CSR Initiatives
