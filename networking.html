Health & Nutrition

The Forum will showcase the tangible and intangible gains that accrue to those private sector organizations that undertake health as part of their CSR initiatives with a special focus on women’s empowerment. It will focus on replicable models on how health issues can be addressed by organizations and how many of such organizations have themselves benefitted from such endeavors.

Primary Education & Literacy

The most critical input or empowering people and giving them the choice of deciding how to best live their lives is education. In fact being fundamentally important, there is great need to increase the level of education in India with a special focus on girl children. This Forum will give emphasis on how organizations can take up education – primary, secondary as also literacy as part of their CSR initiatives and make a strong difference to society and to their business.

Environment

The Forum on Environment will present value generating models across the issue of Environment Conservation as well as showcase how organizations can take up Environment Conservation as part of their CSR initiatives and make a significant contribution on to their business and to society at large

Skills Development

This Networking Forum will highlight the most important issue behind CSR that is Skills Development thereby enhancing employability and livelihood opportunities with a special focus on women. The Forum will help companies to explore undertaking a project that will provide people with a means to earn a livelihood that is perhaps the most essential and basic means for any organization to pay back its debt to society.

CSR & Sustainability

This Forum will focus on
• The origin and significance of CSR
• How CSR and Sustainability will become mutually beneficial to each other thereby positively impacting on the company’s business and brand
• How social equity in partnership projects undertaken by the companies can attain development goals
