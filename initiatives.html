NCN, to meet its vision to empower companies for meaningful social development, is committed to provide platforms in the form of workshops, seminar, conferences, exhibitions and campaigns to sensitize companies on CSR. It would bring together different stakeholders in one platform. It would also partner with relevant agencies both nationally and globally to meet its objectives. Through initiatives such as organizing events, helping with implementation of projects for companies and assessing the impact as also bringing out publications on CSR related matters, NCN would strive to empower companies for building a sustainable future.

Colors of Freedom – a painting drive to spread awareness that Every Child is Special

Denying children with disabilities their right to education had a lifelong impact on learning, achievement and employment opportunities, thereby hindering their potential economic, social and human development. As per the UN report, ‘Out of 2.9 million children with disabilities in India, 990,000 children aged 6 to 14 years (34 percent) are out of school. The percentages are even higher among children with intellectual disabilities (48 percent), speech impairments (36 percent) and multiple disabilities (59 percent).’ To coincide with the 70th Independence Day celebrations, National CSR Network (NCN), Awesomesauce (a PR agency) and Saarthak Educational Society organized a Children’s Painting Drive on the theme “COLORS OF FREEDEOM” that was held on August 13, 2016 in New Delhi to create sensitization through social media and other social handles that being differently abled just posed a challenge, it did not deny capability of an individual. The experiences/ideas shared by children in the form of paintings depicted what freedom meant to them. It helped us to look at freedom from a different lens and spend some time with the amazingly cute children at Sarthak Educational Trust. We hope to reach out to the companies to help them live their dream. We can't help everyone but we believe everyone can help someone to make world a better place.

Meetings

‘MEET & GREET’ the launch and first meeting of NCN

The first meeting of National CSR Network (NCN) – MEET & GREET was organized on June 30, 2016 when the logo of NCN was formally launched. The meeting had a representation by a group of professionals from PSUs, MNCs and private companies. At this event, NCN launched its logo and brochure designed by Fulki Communications Pvt Ltd. Besides these launches, the Networking Forums were also formed, namely, Primary Health, Primary Education and Literacy, Environment and Skills Development. Both Minda Industries Ltd and Macmillan Publishers India Pvt Ltd, shared their CSR initiatives that were well received by the participants.
